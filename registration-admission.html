Admission FAQs

The child should be 3+ years as of June 1, 2024 for Nursery admission. For LKG, the minimum age is 4+, and for UKG it's 5+.

For Nursery to UKG, we conduct an interactive session with the child and parents. For Class 1 onwards, there's a basic assessment in English, Math, and General Awareness.

We maintain a healthy ratio of 25:1 for primary classes and 30:1 for higher classes, with additional support staff for specialized subjects.

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for students from Class 3 onwards. There are also need-based concessions for economically weaker students.

We follow all government guidelines including regular sanitization, temperature checks, and optional hybrid learning models when needed.
